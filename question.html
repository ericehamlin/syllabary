The Syllabary runs on tablets and (in portrait view, cropped) on mobile phones.

It is designed for installation in galleries and for live performance by up to seven readers, ideally for one hour at a time.

Printed versions of the score, in one-off, randomized sequences, may be printed by Molecular Press, when the text is completed, in 2022. Enquiries to www.molecularpress.com
